Types of Green Polyhouses

Vegetable Polyhouse

We use cutting-edge natural technology to grow the highest quality cucumber, capsicum, cherry tomato, and other vegetables. These vegetables are produced in a very good manner and with high production. As a result, we receive a very nice and high price return for these crops.

Key Crops:

  • Coloured Capsicum (Bell Peppers)
  • Cucumber
  • Cherry Tomato
  • Other Premium Vegetables

Flower Polyhouse

We use advanced natural technology to cultivate the highest quality flowers, mostly Dutch roses and gerberas. These flowers are abundantly produced. They have strong demand in Indian and foreign markets. As a result, we receive a very significant return for these flower crops.

Key Flowers:

  • Dutch Roses
  • Gerbera Daisies
  • Premium Cut Flowers

Investment Opportunities

Land by Company

The company owns the highest quality fertilized land bank in several locations around India, particularly in the north. All of the land that we will give you for your ideal poly project is very suitable for poly houses and is completely developed and controlled for poly houses as lock and key.

Leased Land

The company will also give or manage property on lease for you. All of these lands are ideal for poly houses and have all the greatest amenities needed to operate them effectively. Therefore, you can invest less and get the same return without having to buy land.
